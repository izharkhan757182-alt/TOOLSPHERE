How to use the Age Calculator

  1. Step 1. Select your date of birth using the date picker.
  2. Step 2. By default, today's date is used to calculate your age — or pick a different reference date.
  3. Step 3. Your age in years, months and days appears instantly, along with days left until your next birthday.

Frequently asked questions

Does this account for leap years?

Yes, the calculation uses actual calendar dates so leap years are automatically handled correctly.

Can I calculate someone else's age?

Yes — just enter any date of birth, it doesn't have to be your own.
