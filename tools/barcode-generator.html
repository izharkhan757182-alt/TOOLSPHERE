How to use the Barcode Generator

  1. Step 1. Type the text or number you want encoded.
  2. Step 2. Choose a format — CODE128 works for any text, while EAN-13 and UPC require exact digit counts common in retail products.
  3. Step 3. Click Download to save the barcode as a PNG image.

Frequently asked questions

What's the difference between the formats?

CODE128 encodes any letters, numbers or symbols. EAN-13 and UPC are numeric-only retail standards used on product packaging worldwide.

Why does my EAN-13 code show an error?

EAN-13 requires exactly 13 digits (or 12 plus an auto-calculated check digit) — check the length of your input.
