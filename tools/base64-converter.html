Frequently asked questions

What is Base64 used for?

Base64 encodes binary or text data into ASCII characters, commonly used for embedding data in URLs, emails, or JSON.

Is Base64 encryption?

No. Base64 is just an encoding format, not encryption — anyone can decode it, so don't use it to protect sensitive data.
