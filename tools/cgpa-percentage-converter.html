How to use the CGPA to Percentage Converter

  1. Step 1. To convert CGPA to percentage, enter your CGPA (out of 10) on the left.
  2. Step 2. To convert percentage to CGPA, enter your percentage on the right.
  3. Step 3. This uses the commonly accepted formula: Percentage = CGPA × 9.5.

Frequently asked questions

Is the ×9.5 formula universal?

It's the formula recommended by CBSE and used by many Indian universities, but some institutions use their own conversion — check your university's official formula for official documents.

Can CGPA be above 10?

No, CGPA is capped at 10 on this standard scale.
