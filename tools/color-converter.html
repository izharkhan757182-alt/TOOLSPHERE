Frequently asked questions

What's the difference between RGB and HSL?

RGB defines a color by red, green and blue intensity; HSL defines it by hue, saturation and lightness — often more intuitive for adjusting shades.

Can I paste a HEX code without the #?

Yes, the tool accepts both '#7B5CFA' and '7B5CFA' formats.
