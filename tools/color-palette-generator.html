Frequently asked questions

What's the difference between shades and tints?

Shades mix your color with black (darker), while tints mix it with white (lighter) — both keep the same hue.

What's a complementary color?

It's the color directly opposite on the color wheel, often used to create high-contrast accents alongside your base color.
