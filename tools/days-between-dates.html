How to use the Days Between Dates Calculator

  1. Step 1. Pick your start date and end date.
  2. Step 2. The total number of days, weeks, and approximate months appears instantly.
  3. Step 3. Works for both future dates (countdown) and past dates (elapsed time).

Frequently asked questions

Does it matter which date is first?

No, the tool calculates the absolute difference regardless of which date is earlier.

How is 'months' calculated?

It's an approximation based on total days divided by 30.44 (the average month length), useful for a rough estimate.
