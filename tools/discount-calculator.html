How to use the Discount Calculator

  3. Step 3. See exactly how much you save and the final price to pay.

Frequently asked questions

How is the final price calculated?

Final price = Original price × (1 − discount% / 100).

Can I stack two discounts?

This tool handles one discount at a time. For stacked discounts, apply the first discount, then run the result through the calculator again with the second percentage.
