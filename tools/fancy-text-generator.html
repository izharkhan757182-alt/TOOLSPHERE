Frequently asked questions

Will these fonts show correctly everywhere?

These are unicode character variants, not real fonts, so they render on most modern apps and phones, though very old devices may show boxes for some characters.

Does this work for non-English text?

This tool maps Latin letters (A–Z, a–z, 0–9) — other scripts won't be converted.
