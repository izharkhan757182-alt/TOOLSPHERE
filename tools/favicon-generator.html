How to use the Favicon Generator

  1. Step 1. Upload a square image (a logo or icon works best).
  2. Step 2. Favicon versions at 16×16, 32×32, and 180×180 pixels generate automatically.
  3. Step 3. Download each size and add them to your site's <head> with the appropriate <link rel="icon"> tags.

Frequently asked questions

Why do I need multiple sizes?

Different browsers, tabs, and devices (like iOS home screen icons) request different favicon sizes for best display quality.

What format should my favicon be?

PNG is the safest choice for wide compatibility across modern browsers and devices.
