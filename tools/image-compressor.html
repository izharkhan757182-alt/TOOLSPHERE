How to use the Image Compressor

  1. Step 1. Upload a JPG or PNG image from your device.
  2. Step 2. Adjust the quality slider — lower quality means a smaller file size.
  3. Step 3. Compare original vs. compressed size, then click Download.

Frequently asked questions

Is my image uploaded anywhere?

No, compression happens entirely in your browser using the Canvas API — the image never leaves your device.

What format is the output?

The compressed image is saved as JPEG, which compresses well for photos. For images needing transparency, keep quality high or use PNG instead.

Why did the file size not shrink much?

Very simple or already-compressed images may not shrink further. Try a higher-resolution photo or lower quality setting.
