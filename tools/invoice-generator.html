How to use the Invoice Generator

  1. Step 1. Fill in your business name, address, and the client's billing details.
  2. Step 2. Add line items with description, quantity, and rate — amounts and totals calculate automatically.
  3. Step 3. Set a tax percentage if applicable.
  4. Step 4. Click Print / Save as PDF and choose 'Save as PDF' in your browser's print dialog.

Frequently asked questions

Does this store my invoice data?

No, everything stays in your browser. Nothing is uploaded, so make sure to save or print before closing the tab.

Can I add my logo?

This version is text-based for simplicity. You can add your business name prominently in the business name field.

How do I save it as a PDF?

Click Print, then in the print dialog choose 'Save as PDF' as the destination instead of a physical printer.
