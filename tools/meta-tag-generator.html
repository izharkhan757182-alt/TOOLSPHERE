How to use the Meta Tag Generator

Frequently asked questions

What's Open Graph?

Open Graph tags control how your page looks when shared on Facebook, LinkedIn, and other platforms — the preview image, title, and description.

Do I need Twitter Card tags separately?

Most platforms fall back to Open Graph tags, but dedicated Twitter Card tags ensure the best appearance specifically on X/Twitter.
