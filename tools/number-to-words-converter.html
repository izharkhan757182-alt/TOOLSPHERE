How to use the Number to Words Converter

  1. Step 1. Type any whole number.
  2. Step 2. The amount appears in words using the Indian numbering system (lakh, crore) — commonly used on cheques and invoices in India.
  3. Step 3. Copy the result directly into your document.

Frequently asked questions

Does this support decimals for currency (paise)?

This version converts whole numbers. For amounts with paise, round to the nearest rupee or write the paise portion separately.

What's the largest number supported?

Numbers up to 999,99,99,999 (99 crore range) convert correctly.
