How to use the Online Notepad

  1. Step 1. Just start typing — there's nothing to set up.
  2. Step 2. Word count updates live at the bottom.
  3. Step 3. Click Download .txt to save your notes as a text file before closing the tab.

Frequently asked questions

Are my notes saved automatically?

No — this is an in-memory scratchpad. Download your notes as a .txt file before closing or refreshing the page, or they'll be lost.

Is this private?

Yes, nothing you type is sent anywhere or stored on any server.
