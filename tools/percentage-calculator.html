How to use the Percentage Calculator

  1. Step 1. Use the first box to find X% of a number (e.g. 20% of 150).
  2. Step 2. Use the second box to find what percent one number is of another (e.g. 30 out of 200).
  3. Step 3. Use the third box to find the percentage increase or decrease between two numbers.

Frequently asked questions

How is percentage change calculated?

It's calculated as ((New − Old) / Old) × 100. A positive result means an increase, negative means a decrease.

Can I use decimals?

Yes, all three calculators accept decimal numbers.
