How to use the QR Code Generator

  1. Step 1. Type or paste your link, phone number, Wi-Fi text, or any message into the text box.
  2. Step 2. Pick a foreground and background color that fits your brand.
  3. Step 3. Drag the size slider to fit where you'll print or display the code.
  4. Step 4. Click Download PNG and save the file to your device.
  5. Step 5. Test it by scanning with your phone camera before printing or publishing.

Frequently asked questions

Is this QR code generator free?

Yes, completely free with no signup, watermark, or limit on how many codes you can create.

Do the QR codes expire?

No. These are static QR codes — once generated, the image works forever and isn't tied to our servers.

Can I use a custom color?

Yes, choose any foreground and background color. For best scan reliability, keep strong contrast between the two.

What can I put inside a QR code?

URLs, plain text, phone numbers, email addresses, or Wi-Fi credentials all work — anything that fits as text.
