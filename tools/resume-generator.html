How to use the Resume / CV Generator

  1. Step 1. Fill in your name, contact details, and a short summary.
  2. Step 2. List your experience and education, one entry per line, using the comma format shown as an example.
  3. Step 3. Add your skills separated by commas.
  4. Step 4. Click Print / Save as PDF once the preview on the right looks right.

Frequently asked questions

Is my resume data saved anywhere?

No, everything is generated live in your browser. Save or print it before closing the tab, since nothing is stored.

Can I reorder sections?

This version follows a fixed, ATS-friendly order: summary, experience, education, skills — the most commonly expected resume structure.
