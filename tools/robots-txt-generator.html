Frequently asked questions

Where does robots.txt need to go?

It must be placed at the root of your domain, e.g. yoursite.com/robots.txt — it won't work in a subfolder.

Does robots.txt guarantee privacy?

No — it's a request that well-behaved crawlers respect, not a security measure. Never rely on it to hide sensitive content.
