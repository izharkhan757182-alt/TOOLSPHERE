Frequently asked questions

Why is 3999 the maximum?

Standard Roman numerals don't have a clean symbol for 4000 and beyond without special notation, so 3999 (MMMCMXCIX) is the practical upper limit.

Are Roman numerals still used today?

Yes — commonly for clock faces, book chapter numbers, movie sequel numbering, and formal document dates.
