Frequently asked questions

Why do slugs matter for SEO?

Clean, readable URLs help both search engines and users understand what a page is about, and they're easier to share.

Does this remove special characters?

Yes, punctuation and symbols are stripped, spaces become hyphens, and everything is lowercased.
