How to use the Social Media Character Counter

  1. Step 1. Type or paste your caption, tweet, or post into the box.
  2. Step 2. Each platform's limit is shown with how many characters you have left.
  3. Step 3. Red means you're over the limit for that platform — trim your text until it turns green.

Frequently asked questions

Are these limits always accurate?

Limits reflect commonly published platform maximums as of 2026 and can change — always double check against the app if you're right at the edge.

Does this count emojis correctly?

Most emoji count as 2 characters in these platforms' systems; this tool counts by raw character length, which is close but may vary slightly for complex emoji.
