How to use the Speed, Distance & Time Calculator

  1. Step 1. Choose what you want to calculate: speed, distance, or time.
  2. Step 2. Fill in the two known values (using the labels shown).
  3. Step 3. The result calculates automatically using Speed = Distance ÷ Time.

Frequently asked questions

What units does this use?

Enter distance in kilometers and time in hours for speed in km/h — or use consistent units of your choice (e.g. miles and hours for mph).

Can I use minutes instead of hours?

Enter time as a decimal of hours (e.g. 30 minutes = 0.5) for accurate results.
