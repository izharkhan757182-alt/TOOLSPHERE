How to use the Text Diff Checker

  1. Step 1. Paste your original text into the left box.
  2. Step 2. Paste the updated or edited version into the right box.
  3. Step 3. The differences appear below — removed words are struck through in red, added words are highlighted in teal.

Frequently asked questions

What kind of diff is this?

It's a word-level comparison — it highlights words that were added or removed between the two versions.

Can I compare paragraphs of text?

Yes, it works on any length of text, though very long documents are easier to compare a section at a time.
