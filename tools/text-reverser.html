How to use the Text Reverser

  1. Step 1. Type or paste your text into the input box.
  2. Step 2. Choose whether to reverse it letter-by-letter, or reverse the order of the words.
  3. Step 3. The tool also tells you if your text is a palindrome (reads the same forwards and backwards).

Frequently asked questions

What's a palindrome?

A palindrome is text that reads the same forwards and backwards, ignoring spaces, punctuation, and capitalization — like 'racecar' or 'A man a plan a canal Panama'.

What's the difference between the two reverse modes?

'Reverse letters' flips the entire string character by character. 'Reverse word order' keeps each word intact but reverses their sequence.
