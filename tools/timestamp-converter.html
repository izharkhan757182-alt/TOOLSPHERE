How to use the Timestamp Converter

  1. Step 1. To convert a timestamp to a date, paste the Unix timestamp (in seconds) into the left box.
  2. Step 2. To convert a date to a timestamp, type a date/time on the right in the format YYYY-MM-DDTHH:MM, or click 'Use current time'.
  3. Step 3. Both local time and UTC are shown for the timestamp-to-date conversion.

Frequently asked questions

What is a Unix timestamp?

It's the number of seconds elapsed since January 1, 1970 (UTC), widely used in programming and databases to represent a point in time.

Does this handle milliseconds?

This tool expects seconds. If your timestamp is in milliseconds, divide it by 1000 first.
