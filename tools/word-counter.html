Frequently asked questions

Does this tool store my text?

No. Everything is counted locally in your browser — nothing you type is uploaded or saved anywhere.

How is reading time calculated?

We estimate reading time at roughly 200 words per minute, a common average adult reading speed.

What counts as a sentence?

Any text segment ending in a period, question mark, or exclamation mark is counted as one sentence.
